Alarm

Murdoch campus is in chaos. Emails are flying, QR posters plaster walls, and IT phones are exploding. Murdoch IT bursts in: The Perverted Hacker, cyber villain is here! Hes using phishing emails and dodgy QR codes. Hero, please help! You can investigate: [[Inbox (phishing email)->Inbox]] [[Canteen (QR poster)->Canteen]] [[Suspicious tinyurl message->Tinyurl]]

Inbox

You get an email: From: IT Support <it-support@murdoch-helpdesk.co> Subject: URGENT: Reset in 10 minutes or lose Wi-Fi Dear User, Unusual activity detected. Please reset your password immediately. [Reset Now] Choices: [[Click the button (enter credentials)->Compromised]] [[Inspect sender + headers->InspectEmail]] [[Delete without reporting->IgnoreEmail]]

Canteen

At the canteen wall: FREE NUGGETS FOR LIFE Scan me! Your classmate, Benny, who happens to be beside you whispered: Bro, confirm legit. Choices: [[Scan immediately->Quished]] [[Preview link with QR tool->PreviewQR]] [[Rip poster off and send to security->HeroEndPoster]]

Tinyurl

You get a message: Verify your account here: https://tinyurl.com/murdoch-reset Choices: [[Click it directly->BadTinyurl]] [[Use a preview tool to check destination->PreviewTinyurl]] [[Google the final domain->PreviewTinyurl]]

Compromised

You entered your password. Seconds later, your account spams everyone with Princes Inheritance scams. Murdoch IT facepalms: You just gave The Perverted Hacker a free pass. Learning: Never click & type credentials into unverified pages. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

InspectEmail

You hover: link = murdoch-helpdesk.co (not murdoch.edu.au). Choices: [[Report to IT with headers->HeroEndEmail]] [[Ignore and close window->IgnoreEmail]]

IgnoreEmail

You delete it silently. A day later, half your class is locked out. Murdoch IT sighs: If only you had reported Learning: Deleting solving. Always report. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

BadTinyurl

You click. Fake login page appears. You enter details, and The Perverted Hacker laughs: Another one! Learning: Shortened links hide danger. Always preview. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

PreviewTinyurl

You preview. Destination = murdoch-helpdesk.co. Google says its a known phishing site. You report it. Murdoch IT: Legend! We blocked the whole campaign. Learning: Preview + verify domains before trusting links. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

HeroEndEmail

Murdoch IT bursts in with donuts: You reported properly and saved us! This phish is blocked. Learning: Always check sender domain + headers, and report instead of ignoring. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

Quished

You scan. Phone buzzes: Subscribed to Evil Newsletter. Classmates start getting spam from your number. Learning: Scanning QR blindly is dangerous. Preview first. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.

PreviewQR

Preview shows murdoch-v0uchers.co (with a zero). Choices: [[Report suspicious link to IT->HeroEndPoster]] [[Ignore typo and visit site->Quished]]

HeroEndPoster

You report and security removes the posters. Murdoch IT: Well done! Posters gone, scam blocked. Learning: Always preview QR links and report dodgy posters. Proceed to stairs on the right Malware room.
